We are an ever-expanding collection of teenage entrepreneurs eager to incite real and meaningful change within our communities. Are you ready for the challenge?

Please note that all applications, with the exception of the Executive Team application, are reviewed on a rolling-basis.

Chapter Leader

Apply

Do you have a passion to learn and lead others? If so, you're a perfect fit to become a Protostar chapter leader!

Chapter leaders are responsible for establishing a Protostar club at their schools, leading club meetings, teaching peers about the entrepreneurial process through our hands-on curriculum, and supporting them in building their companies. Chapter leaders are also allowed (and encouraged) to participate in the competition!

Mentor/Adviser

Apply

Our mentor & mentee program, The Hub, relies on the generous support of advisers and mentors.

Whether you are a programmer, artist, college student, or entrepreneur, your diverse perspective helps our students create stronger companies. Advisers are matched up with a team at the start of the program and offer them feedback/advice on all phases of their entrepreneurial journey.

Time commitment is no more than an hour bi-weekly and means of communication is up to the mentor/advisor's preference. This is the perfect option if you would like to support our students remotely!

Regional Director

Apply

Do you love organizing events and expanding your connections? If so, apply to be a Protostar Regional Director in your city today!

Regional Directors are responsible for overseeing all Protostar chapters in their cities. They are responsible for organizing local events with the support of the Protostar Executive Team, expanding Protostar in their city, engaging with other Protostar students, and virtually transforming the startup ecosystem in their city! Intrigued? Apply today!
