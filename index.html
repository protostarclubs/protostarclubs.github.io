Our Mission.

Collaboration, creativity, and diversity - a collection of makers, doers, artists, programmers, and other creatives. Oh. Did we mention we're teenagers too?

The world is in a dire need for forward-thinking problem solvers who are willing to challenge decades of traditional thinking in order to tackle complex issues that are hindering the progression of humanity in brand new innovative ways. In the midst of a technological revolution, entrepreneurs will be the catalyst for change towards today's most complex challenges. Yet, the field faces a shortage in underrepresented individuals (either in age, gender, or ethnicity) and is generally accompanied by the unwelcoming stigma of "being a businessman". Protostar strives to change that. Our mission is two-fold: Redefine "entrepreneurship" and transform the startup ecosystem to become more inclusive. Today's modern-day definition of "entrepreneurship" is outdated. It reflects the misconception that "entrepreneurship" is a verb meaning "to create a business", when in reality it's much more than that. It's a mindset. By providing teenage entrepreneurs the platform and tools to develop their creativity and network with other like-minded peers, we hope to inspire students with the tenacity to build and pursue their own viable startups, molded to fit their own ideal of the world. The focus of our program is on taking action, learning from results, helping students become successful entrepreneurs, and most importantly, having fun.
